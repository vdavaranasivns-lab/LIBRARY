Digital Libraries & Free E-Resources

Hand-picked open resources for students, researchers and lifelong learners — click any card to visit the library.

National Repository

National Digital Library of India (NDLI)

A virtual repository of learning resources — textbooks, articles, video lectures, audiobooks and more for every learner. Developed and maintained by IIT Kharagpur.

ndl.iitkgp.ac.in
eBooks

Project Gutenberg

A volunteer effort founded in 1971 to digitize and archive cultural works. Over 75,000 free eBooks — classics of world literature, free to read and download.

gutenberg.org
Digital Archive

Internet Archive

A non-profit library of millions of free books, movies, music, software and websites — including a huge collection of digitized Indian texts.

archive.org
Indian Heritage

Ved Puran

Free downloads of the Vedas, Puranas and other sacred Sanskrit-Hindi texts for readers, students and researchers of Indian scriptures.

vedpuran.net
Indian Heritage

Vedic Heritage Portal

Government of India portal presenting Vedic texts, audio-video recordings of recitations and knowledge of India's Vedic tradition in one place.

vedicheritage.gov.in
Indian Heritage

Jain eLibrary

A vast collection of Jain literature with the Jain Quantum search engine, which lets you search within the full text of thousands of Jain works.

jainelibrary.org
Manuscripts

National Mission for Manuscripts (NAMAMI)

India's national mission to survey, document, conserve and digitize the country's vast manuscript wealth and make it accessible to all.

namami.gov.in
हिंदी

Hindi Samay — World of Hindi

हिंदी का रचना संसार — a rich online collection of Hindi literature: stories, novels, poetry, essays and criticism from classic and modern writers.

hindisamay.com
हिंदी काव्य

Kavita Kosh

The largest online treasury of Hindi and other Indian-language poetry — from Kabir and Tulsidas to contemporary poets, free for everyone.

kavitakosh.org
हिंदी

Hindwi

A beautifully curated platform for Hindi poetry and prose — dohas, ghazals, kavita and stories with meanings and audio, by the Rekhta Foundation.

hindwi.org
संस्कृत

Sanskrit.nic.in — Sanskrit Theses

Official portal for Sanskrit studies with access to Sanskrit theses, publications and resources for scholars and students of the language.

sanskrit.nic.in
संस्कृत

Sanskrit eBooks

Thousands of free downloadable Sanskrit eBooks — scriptures, stotras, grammar, literature and rare digitized volumes.

sanskritebooks.org
اردو • Urdu

Rekhta

The world's largest website for Urdu literature — 23,060+ Urdu eBooks plus ghazals, nazms and shayari with Devanagari and Roman scripts.

rekhta.org
Open Access Books

DOAB — Directory of Open Access Books

33,378 academic, peer-reviewed open-access books from 406 publishers — free to read and download for research and study.

doabooks.org
Journals

DOAJ — Directory of Open Access Journals

A community-curated index of high-quality, open-access, peer-reviewed journals across every subject and language of the world.

doaj.org
Journals

NOPR — NISCPR Online Periodicals Repository

Open repository of research journals published by CSIR–NISCPR, covering science, technology and traditional knowledge of India.

nopr.niscpr.res.in
Repositories

OpenDOAR

A global directory of open-access institutional repositories — discover research collections from universities and institutes worldwide.

opendoar.org
Open Courseware

Yale Open Courses

Free and open access to introductory courses taught by distinguished professors of Yale University — lectures, videos and materials.

oyc.yale.edu
Open Courseware

eGyanKosh

A national digital repository to store, index, preserve and share digital learning resources developed by open and distance learning institutions of India.

egyankosh.ac.in
Theses

Shodhganga

INFLIBNET's reservoir of Indian theses and dissertations — full-text PhD research from universities across the country, open to all.

shodhganga.inflibnet.ac.in
